25- A Suku, a —ala, eci sokolola


1.	
	A Suku, a —ala, eci sokolola
	Evi via tunga ovaka ove,
	Kilu loposi oviluvo viove
	Via molehisa unene wove.

		Utima wange u sivaya Suku  / bis
		Omo eye Muele, eye Fu       /  bis

2.	
	Nda sokolola ndomo Suku a tuma
	Omolahe oku njovola,
	Ndomo a fila ame kekulusu,
	Ci Òomohisa ca lua enene!

3.	
	Kuenje eci iya kumue lovangelo
	Oku tu tuala kilu kulamba,
	Ha petamela kolomai viahe,
	Ha linga siti, a —ala, ove Fu.
