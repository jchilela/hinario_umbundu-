124- Ku ka linge huti ame ndumalehe


	
	Ci pita vutima wove, Suku o ci lete (ter)
		Ove okuetu enju kokuake.

1.	
	Ku ka linge huti ame ndumalehe.
	Evi via —ala ndi vi sanda noke.
	—ala Yesu, okuetu, love wa ku kovonga,
	Ove, okuetu, enju kokuahe.

2. 	
	Ku ka linge huti, ame ndukulu ale,
	Evi via —ala ndi vi sia lomala.
	—ala yesu, okuetu, love wa ku kovonga,
	Ove, okuetu, enju kokuahe.

3. 	
	Osimbu kua citilue eye wa ku kuliha,
	Omuenyo wove uli povaka ahe.
	Eye o ku sole kuenda o ku lava.
	Ove, okuetu, enju kokuahe.
