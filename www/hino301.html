301- Ove a Yesu, ukuahenda


1.	
	Ove a Yesu, ukuahenda,
	Wa ndingila Ungombo uwa.
	Ove okasi locisola
	Haive ukuacali ca lua
	Ove usongui wolomeke,
	Usakuli wolombeyi.
	Ndo songuile kuwa wove.
	Ndo sakule utima wange.



2.	
	Ove Ulolo liovitima;
    Ove lika hu kolela.
	Ku ka njanduluke, a Yesu;
	Lika liange ndi nyelela.
	Kupi vali sanga ongusu
	Te kokuove, Unjovoli?	
	Kolohali lokayonjo
	Linga otevele yange.



3.	
	Ove a Yesu, Umuenyo wange, 
	Haive Ukuasi ka wimuha.  	
	Si ka linge vali osuke;
	Ndinge ohuasi yekolelo.
	Ove wa pua evelo liosi;
	Ame nda pua pe esunga.
	Ndo nyimule ekambo liosi.
	Ndo sambuise esunga liove.
