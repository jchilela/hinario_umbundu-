54- Nda tu sokolola Onduko ya kemla


1.	
	Nda tu sokolola Onduko ya kem„la
	Kulo Koluali luosi,
	—ala, —ala, —ala, —ala, —ala Yesu,
	Suku Ndaka wa sovola ilu losi
	kuenda omanu lovina viosi,
	—ala, —ala, —ala, —ala, —ala Yesu,
	Tu sambiliyi, tu sivayi —ala.
	—ala Yesu Osoma hu,
	Weya voluali ndosuke
	Omola a Suku, ha yovola omanu.


		A sivayiwe, a sivayiwe,
		—ala a sivayiwe! (bis)
		Tu ku sivaya ndati,
		a —ala Ukuamuenyo? (bis)
		Tuimbe onumbi, a —ala,
		ndomo tu ku sivaya. (bis)
		Aleluya! (10 x)

2.	
	Nda tu sokolola Onjovoli ya kem„la
	Kulo koluali luosi
	—ala, —ala, —ala, —ala, —ala Yesu,
	Eye lika o tela oku eca eyovo
	lia suapo lepanduluko,
	—ala, —ala, —ala, —ala, —ala Yesu,
	Helie o pondola oku liseteka la Yesu
	Onjovoli yenda hu?
	Wa tu lunduila unyali wetu
	Wa pamele, Satana, kuenda okufa.

3.	
	Nda tu sokolola Osoma yi ka viala
	Kilu loposi otembo yosi,
	—ala, —ala, —ala, —ala, —ala Yesu,
	Kotembo eya o ka lundula ovoviali
	osi okilu lieve,
	—ala, —ala, —ala, —ala, —ala Yesu,
	Wosi o pat„la Ondaka yaíe la Suku Isia,
	O ka pumba omuenyo hu.
	U o liyaka londaka yaye,
	Kaliye eye o ka nyolehapo.
