1.	
	Evi violuali via huvika enene;
	Cilo njongola evi viokovailu;
	Kuenje eci natelela eviho liange
	Njevite elaleko liocisola.


2.	
	Ndimbuka ondaka yonjovoli yange.
	Eye o mayula leka lia tomiwa.
	Osonde yaye oyo yi njelisa;
	Okufa kuaye oko ku njovola.


3.	
	Ocili, a Yesu, ove usunga liange;
	Wa lituikile akandu ange osi.
	Cove olosongo; cange ekolowa;
	Cove okufa; cange omuenyo ko pui.


4.	
	A Ñala yange, ndinge embombe liove;
	Ñale lutima woku felevela.
	Oco nyiÒile noke lonjolela
	Vonjo wa yi mongiyila kimbo liove.
