189- Ove a —ala, ove Usongui


1.	
	Ove a —ala, ove Usongui,
	Ndo songuile kuosi o panga.
	Nyihe okuokuo, hu kuatelele,
	Momo mita vekalosoko.
	Ove o njupa ocilulumu,
	Nda hu yeva, a Spiritu.
	O maÒinya huti, Enju.
	Hu songuila; ku ka yokoke.

2.	
	Kala lame, a —ala yohenda,
	Si endi love kuosi o songuila.
	Ku ka siepo; Òuete usumba;
	Owelema wa njokokisa.
	Kulo kilu lieve mapata,
	Sanga haiko ndi nyelela.
	Ndo maÒinye huti, Enju.
	Hu songuila; ku ka yokoke.
	
3.	
	Ove, a —ala, ove Unjuli
	Wa yula ale unene weyambo,
	Eci njoka olui luokufa
	Olusipa ka lu ngambata.
	O njowisa, mitila ciwa
	Te kofeka yohuminyo.
	Wa ndaleka, huti, Enju,
	Hu songuila; ku ka yokoke.
