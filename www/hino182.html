182- A Ñala Yesu


1.	
	A —ala Yesu
    Ndeya kokuove.
    A —ala yange
    Ndeya kokuove.

		Ndeya letimba liange
		Ndeya lomuenyo wange.
		Ndeya kokuove.
		Ngecele akandu ange.
		Mopele kuvi wosi.
		Ndeya kokuove.

2.  
	A —ala, akandu ange
    A nema enene.
    Njongola oku yelisua
    Vemehi liosonde.
3.  
	A  —ala, wa lulika
    Ovina viosi.
    Wa lisula lomunu.
    Wa ku setehala.

4.  
	A —ala, oluali lulo
    Ndopo lu sulila.
    Kuenda asanju oluali
    Ndopo a sulilavo.

5.  
	A —ala ndopo wiya
    Oku tu upilila.
    A —ala eteke wiya,
    Wiya lovalende.
