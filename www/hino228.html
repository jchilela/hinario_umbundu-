228- Yola yola, momo Yesu o ku sole



 		(v) Yola yola, ota memenako,
		Yola yola, momo Yesu o ku sole!

1.	
	Yola yola, momo Yesu o ku sole,
	Eye wa kuliha upongo wove.
	Yola yola, momo u wa ku vala ndekamba
	Eye Isia ka pui.

2.	
	Yola yola, momo Yesu o ku sole,
	Eye wa kuliha yove.
	Epute liove omanu va li yeva Òo oku leha,
	Pu„i Yesu o li yeva oku vala.
3.	
	Yola yola, momo Yesu o ku sole,
	Eye wa kiliha ohali yove.
	Yola yola, momo eci ca veta upindi,
	Mbumbuangolo ka co sile.

4.	
	 Yola yola, momo Yesu o ku sole,
	Oku ku sokolola eye ka pekela.
	Wa ku kapa kolongolo viaye,
	Memenako momo eye o ku susumisa.
