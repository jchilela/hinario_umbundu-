299- Kokuove lika a Yesu


1.	
	Kokuove lika a Yesu,
	mola ombembua ya Suku
	Omanu ka va tela pua oku yi nyeha.
	Kuli ulamba, huti Òo uwa, wa posoka u tala,
	Pu„i wesanda, u sulila, u pumbisa utima.

2.	
	Kokuove lika a Yesu, mola utanya wenda hu,
	Owo u songuila kovailu, ka wimuna mbu.
	NdaÒo ayonjo a lua a njonja,
	pamue omanu va nambela
	Haimo kokuove nilila, lacimue mumbuka.

3.	
	Kokuove lika a Yesu, mola esanju liocili,
	Momo o li kumue lame ndaÒo ohali ya lua.
	Kokuove lika mola cosi ndi ci sukila palo posi.
	A —ala, kuoha ka kuli umue wa lisoka love.

4.	
	Kokuove lika a Yesu, mola omuenyo wenda hu,
	Momo upange weyovo ove wo mala pe.
	Ove lika hu sanjukila toke o nyiÒisa kovailu,
	Leci hu mola hu panduila omo wa mopela.
