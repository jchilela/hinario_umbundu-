221- Konjila yetu yokovailu


 1.	
 	Konjila yetu yokovailu
	Nda tua kava, tu ivaluka
	Ohuminyo ya Suku yiti,
	Hu songuila lovaso.

		Hu songuila, hu hu songuila,
		Hu songuila lovaso.
		Konjila yiwa yokovailu,
		Hu songuila kovaso.

2.	
	Nda ayonjo a ku kuata,
	Nda wa kamba ongusu,
	Sokolola ondaka yiti,
	Hu songuila lovaso.

3.	
	Efulilo eci liya
	Kuenje o tula omuenyo,
    Suku o ku paninya hati,
    Hu songuila lovaso.
