261- A Yesu, ndimeke


1.	
	A Yesu, ndimeke;
	Ndo songuile.
	Utanya u soka nduteke
	Te nda ove o ngendisa.
	Ndo songuile.
	A Yesu, ndimeke.
 

2.	
	A Yesu, nda huka;
	Ndo mindise.
	Kondaka yove njevako.
	Ndimeme, ove Ungombo.
	Ndo mindise.
	A Yesu, nda huka.


3.	
	A Yesu, ndisuke;
	Ndo nekule.
	Ndo numbuluile lohenda 
	Ovina akandu a nyolela.
	Ndo nekule.
	A Yesu, ndisuke.

 
4.	
	A Yesu, hu sole;
	Ndo Òuatise.
	Upeke, ahuku lusuke
	Ka vi ka nepe kokuove.
	Ndo Òuatise.
	A Yesu, hu sole.
