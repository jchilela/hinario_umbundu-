203- A Yesu Ukamba liokutima


1.	
	A Yesu Ukamba liokutima,	
	Kokuo kuove Òunamela,
	Vonjila yove omo ngendela
	Ove hu kolela.


2.	
	Ukamba wove u sanjuisa,
	Lacimue ciwa o mumbisa,
	Cosi nda suka ove o ci muisa
	Hu kolela, a —ala.


3.	
	NdaÒo ovina via lua vi njonja,
	Lomanu vosi va nambela
	Haimo lepandi lonjolela,
	Hu kolela, a —ala.


4.	
	NdaÒo utima u nukoka,
	NdaÒo ongusu yi nepuluka
	Haimo ha mola oko kua koka,
	Hu kolela, a —ala.
