308- Ndimeke, a Suku, nda loÒa


1.	
	Ndimeke, a Suku, nda loÒa	
	Vonjila ya sanjavala.
	Ovaka ove a niule;
	A songuile.

2.	
	NdaÒo nda linga via senga,
	Nda putua tupu kutima,
	Ovaka ove a fina
	A songuile.

3.	
	NdaÒo nda ambata ca lela, 
	NdaÒo nda ambata ci lema, 
	Ovaka ove a pama 
	A songuile.

4.	
	NdaÒo nda sanga epese,
	NdaÒo nda suka ipako,
	Ovaka ove ohenda
	A songuile.

5.	
	NdaÒo nda nyehua, ndaÒo fa,
	Mitila kilu kulamba.
	Ovaka ove, a Suku,
	A songuile.
