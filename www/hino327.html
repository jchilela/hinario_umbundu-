327- O linga ovikomo a Cime cange


1.	
	O linga ovikomo a Cime cange,
	Wa ndinga ohenda ya lua;
	Vewula liesenje o salamisa,
	O muisa evi via Òamba.


		Vewula liesenje o salamisa
		Vekango eye undembo,
		O mbindikiyamo, o ndembeleka,
		O njupa olukavelo,
		O njupa olukavelo.


2.	
	O linga ovikomo a Cime cange,
	Ca nema o ci nambula,
	Kutima o Òolisa, si sondoloka,
	Longusu o numbulula.


3.	
	Eteke keteke o sukumuila
	Ovasumuluho a lua,
	A Òokela esanju lioku imbaimba
	Isungo ku Suku yange.


4.	
	Eteke ho sanga kalende o kilu,
	Ha wala ulamba uwa,
	Omo a njovola ho tumbangiya,
	Ho panduila ohenda yahe.
