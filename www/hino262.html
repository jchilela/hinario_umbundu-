262- A Yesu, njivaluke


1.	
	A Yesu, njivaluke;
	Mopele kuvi wange.
	Njulise kovayonjo osi.
	Nyihe onjelo yove.

 
2.	
	A Yesu, njivaluke
	Eci nda sakalala.
	Nda ovanyali va njakisa
	Ndingile otevele.


3.	
	A Yesu, njivaluke
	Eci ongusu ya mua.
	Ohenda yove yi Òuatise
	Kuenda yi Òavuluise.


4.	
	A Yesu, njivaluke
	Eci nda linyelisa.
	Nda owelema wa sitika
	Songuile leka liove.


5.	
	A Yesu, njivaluke
	Eci ohali ya lua.
	Pokati kevi vi saluisa
	Ndo Òaile ocituke.


6.	
	A Yesu, njivaluke
	Eci mbelela okufa.
	Ndo Òuate peka; mitilise
	Kimbo liove liesanju.
