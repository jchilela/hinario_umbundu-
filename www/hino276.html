276- A Yesu, a Ñala, hu vialeka


1.	
	A Yesu, a Ñala, hu vialeka,
	Hu lingi soma yange,
	Oco vutima wange o viale,
	Haimo o linga Fu.

		Si ka ivaleko ekulusu.
		Si ka ivaleko ohali yove.
		Ohenda yove yi Òokele
		Utima woku ku vumba.


2.  
	Hu kuame kumue lolondonge
	Muna vo Gesemane;
	Ndimbukilemo ohali yove;
	Ndilile upongo wove.


3.	
	Ndeci Maria, haico ame
	Ngende keyambo liove;
	Ndimbukileko unene wove
	Wa yula lawo okufa.


4.	
	Nyihe ongusu yoku ambata
	Omindikiso yange,
	Ha siu ku kuama locisola
	Kuosi ove o songuila.
