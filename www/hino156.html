156- Nda kaile ndomeme ya tanãyala


1.	
	Nda kaile ndomeme ya tanãyala,
	Vocunda ame sia solelemo;
	Nda linga ahuku nda nyelela muele
	Sia tavele kondaka yungombo.

		Yesu wa ndandula kuenje wa sanga
		Loku lambuka, nda soka okufa,
		Yu Wa mandeka lukolo wocila.

2.	
	Tunde nda tava, Yesu Ungombo wange,
	Utima wange wo sanjukila
	Wa njelisa kono yosonde yahe
	Utima wange wa mbeluisa pe.

3.	
	U wa nyelela Yesu u vanjiliya,
	Ha sangi omeme ya tun„yala,
	Kocunda cahe eye u songuila
	Haiko a lava vosi va tava.
