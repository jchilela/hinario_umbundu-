240- Popia, a Cime, ukuenje o yeva


 1.	
 	Popia, a Cime, ukuenje o yeva;
	Ndi tailili ondaka.
	Njongola oku yi yeva:
	Yi tukula, a Cime.
	—asi loku lavoka
	Eci ove o mbanguisa.


2.	
	Nukule londuko, a Cime,
	Ndimbuke ondaka yove.
	Popia, oco njayuleko
	Lohandokua ya pi„la.
	Kuosi o songuila, a Cime,
	Haiko ame njilila.


3.	
	Situluile ciwa ondaka;
	Si ka tunde lahuku.
	Popia a Cime, momo o ci
	—asi longeva love.
	Vutima nda sukila.
	Sumuluise, a Cime.


4.	
	Popia, a Cime, ndo Òuatise,
	Oco hu lamelele,
	Loku pokola kondaka
	Yosi o handeleka.
	Ndo njevise, a Cime,
	Cosi o vangula lame.
