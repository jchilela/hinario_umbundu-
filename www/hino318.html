318- Ucikepa cange, a Ñala


1.	
	Ucikepa cange, a Ñala,
	Lacimue nda sukila,
	Kovongende ange osi,
	Ñuatise oku enda love.

		Ocituke love, ocituke love, 
		Kovongende ange osi,
    	Ñuatise oku enda love.

2.	
	Si lombela alau oluali,
	Pamue oku kemala,
	Nalavaya Òo-o lepandi,
	Cineme ove o li lame.

		Ocituke love, ocituke love, 
		Nalavaya Òo-o lepandi. 
        Cineme ove o li lame.

3.	
	Vomaka yilo songuile,
	Njowise kolui luokufa,
	Mitilise te kombundi,
	NyiÒila kumue love.

	    Ocituke love, ocituke love,
	   	Eci mitila kombundi,
	   	NyiÒila kumue love.
