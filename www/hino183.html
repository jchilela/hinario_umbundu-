183- Njiya komindikiso


1.			
	Njiya komindikiso,
	Nda lambuka kutima,
	Nda sanda eyovo,
	Cilo a —ala mopele.

		Ove nda ku kolela,
		A kameme ka file,
		Kulo ame Òekama,
		Cilo a —ala mopele.

2. 
	Nda ku yongola, a Yesu,
	Haimo ayonjo a njula,
	A nateka osimbu,
	Cilo a —ala mopele.

3.	
	—olela olohuminyo;
	Nda yovoka losonde,
	Ame ya mesekela.
	Cilo a —ala mopele.
