302- Hu kolela, a Nãla Yesu


1.	
	Hu kolela, a Nãla Yesu,
	Lika wove nı,
	Oco mole eyovo lina
	Li enda hu.


2.	
	Hu kolela, wa ngecela
	Akandu a nema;
	Muise unu ndinge ohenda,
	Ya lova.


3.	
	Hu kolela ndo njelise
	Nãsi leviho,
	Hu kolela, ndo nuwise
	Via Suku.


4.	
	Ove Usungu hu kolela
	Hã ku songuila;
	Ove Uhuasi o ngavela
	Via n„mba.
