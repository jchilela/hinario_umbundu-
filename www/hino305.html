305- Nda limbuka esanju, a Ñala


1.	
		Nda limbuka esanju, a Ñala,
		Ove o muisa ocali.
		Ukuesanju lia kanguka,
		Ndo sanjuise, lame le.
		Lame le. Lame le.
		Ndo sanjuise, lame le.

	
2.	
		Ku ka njanduluke, a Tate;
		Ndukuakandu ocili.
		Si siluila ocali cove.
		Ndinge ohenda, lame le.
		Lame le. Lame le.
		Ndinge ohenda, lame le.


3.	
		Ku ka mitahale, a Yesu;
		Hu kolela. Hu sole.
		Nda livela eyovo liove.
		Ndo njovole, lame le.
		Lame le. Lame le.
		Ndo njovole, lame le.


4.	
		Ku ka siepo, a Espiritu,
		Omo Òasi lupeke.
		Ove muele Uyelisi;
		Ndo njelise, lame le.
		Lame le. Lame le.
		Ndo njelise, lame le.
